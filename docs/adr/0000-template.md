# ADR-0000: Short title of the decision

- **Status:** Proposed
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Deciders:** (who made the call)
- **Supersedes / Superseded by:** (ADR number, or none)

## Context

What is the problem or force that requires a decision? Describe the constraints - the
project's rules (zero runtime dependencies, MVP scope), the alternatives on the table, and
why this needs deciding now. State facts, not the conclusion.

## Decision

The decision, stated in one or two sentences in the active voice: "We will ...". Follow
with the key specifics of *how*.

## Consequences

What becomes easier, and what becomes harder or is now off the table. Include the
tradeoffs we knowingly accept and any follow-up work this creates.

### Alternatives considered

- **Option A** - why not.
- **Option B** - why not.
